About Us

Kamala Polyclinic ®

Kamala Polyclinic has been set up at chandmari, Opposite Atul Krishna girls School, Sonarpur Bazaar, Kolkata with a broad objective to offer the best possible health solutions with allmost care to a vast majority of people at minimum prices. There are numerous number of people who cannot go for check up to a better place due to their financial problems. Kamala Polyclinic comes with all the essential services in order to provide best healthcare solutions to the neediest and poorest of the local population at an affordable cost. We have a brilliant team of doctors and associate to serve all kinds of patients suffering from minor ailment like fever to major ones like diabetes with efficiency.
