  • Crime Prevention & Control

    Purba Medinipur District Police is committed to deliver efficient, effective, pro-active and quality police service to its citizens. We ensure the rule of law without fear or favour. We are committed for prevention and detection of crime and taking strong action against anti-social and organized crime. Prompt and compassionate response to every call of citizens in distress is our priority.

  • Law & Order Situation Management

    The Police force in the district is entrusted with the responsibility of maintenance of public order and peace. The preventive and prompt action by the district police is the key of our success. All permitted political meetings/procession/agitation etc, are covered as per law. In adverse situations, we are equipped with our specialised forces like, District Armed Police and Rapid Action Force. We are bound to maintain peace and tranquillity in the society.

  • Crowd Management

    The crowd gathering in form of Mela, religious festivals like Durga Puja, Muharram etc are planned in advance in form of traffic arrangement, steps to avoid any man-made disaster, prevent crime at such places and well executed from time to time on regular basis. The gathering in form of public rally addressed by political leaders/VIP are planned well in advance to provide the safety and security to VIPs and smooth passage for public in form of efficient traffic management and avoiding any adverse situation. We are committed to ensure that people exercise the right of public assembly with as per law.

    • To save lives
    • To prevent escalation of casualty
    • To relieve suffering
    • To safeguard the social fabric
    • To protect property
    • To facilitate criminal investigation as well as departmental, public or judicial enquiry, if any ordered
    • To restore normalcy as soon as possible.

  • Traffic management

    About Traffic of Purba Medinipur
    PurbaMedinipur District has more than 150 km of National Highway, NH-41 (52 km) from Kolaghat to Haldia, NH-6 (26 km) from Kolaghat to Srirampur (Panskura) and NH-116B (90 km) from Sridharpur More (Nandakumar) to Digha. These highways connect Kolkata to many attractive tourist spots like Digha, Mandermoni, Shankarpur, Tajpur etc and important port as well as Industrial Hub like Haldia. Consequently, many passenger vehicles as well as goods vehicles ply through this district and the District Traffic Police has to play a very challenging role to ensure free flow of vehicular traffic, specifically during tourist seasons and we are performing this task with much zeal, dedication and responsibility. The District Traffic Police has taken many initiatives like installation of Cautionary, Informatory and Mandatory Road signages, introduction of Highway Patrol Vans fitted with Modern Cameras. The installation of CCTV Cameras with special features like NPR and RLVD is going on. Traffic Police has been observing Safe Drive Save Life campaign by holding Awareness Programmes among public by road show, miking, helmet distribution, rakshabandhan and other initiative like “No Helmet, No Petrol” has also been taken. The MV act violators have also been prosecuted. All efforts are being taken to ensure smooth movement of traffic all over the districts and reduce road accidents. This website will act as source of correct information related to District Traffic. Suggestions/Opinions/Feedback/Complaint, if any, may be sent through this website.

  • Missing and recovery of the child/person

    When a child/ person is missing from any one’s home, the family member of the missing children/person should inform the matter to the concerned police station with the following information/documents.

    • The photo of the missing children/ person.
    • Mentioning Height/weight/colour of the missing children/ person in the complaint. Wearing apparel during the time of missing.
    • Any identification mark of the missing children/ person should be mention in the complaint.
    • Actual date & time of missing.

    If any person finds any child without any guardian/ person in need of help, moving at the bus stop/Railway station or any public place, is requested to inform the matter to the nearest police station and also inform District Police Control Room (03228-269797).
