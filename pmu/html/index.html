    Project Monitoring Unit (PMU)

    The Project Monitoring Unit (PMU) undertakes various surveys on state government schemes for impact assessment and identifying underdeveloped blocks and villages in West Bengal. This website will serve as a public-facing platform to showcase PMU activities, provide real-time data insights, and enable citizen participation in governance.

    Objectives

    • To provide transparency in government scheme monitoring.
    • To facilitate real-time data access and impact assessment.
    • To engage citizens in governance through feedback and participation.
    • To integrate with existing government databases for seamless data sharing.

    Overview of PMU’s

    Vision

    To drive efficiency, transparency, and accountability in project execution by leveraging data-driven insights, innovative technologies, and strategic oversight, ensuring sustainable and impactful project outcomes.
