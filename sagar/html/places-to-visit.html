DALA ARCADE

A place near to the Kapil Muni Temple where pilgrims can buy essential commodities for Puja and other souvenir products. The Dala Aracde consists of 120 nos. of shops in 3 blocks and it is the ideal place for picking up gifts for your near and dear ones.

MYTHOLOGICAL MURAL AT DALA ARCADE

The Mythological Murals at Dala Arcade takes us through the events following the Mythology of Saint Kapil and gives us a clear glimpse of how Bhagirath, the grandson of Raja Sagar brought Devi Ganga from heaven to earth and secured his forefathers a place

SCHEME PARK

A park highlighting different flagship programmes of the Government of West Bengal for benefitting the residents of the State. Common people visiting this place will be conscious about these social security schemes of the Government of West Bengal.

DANCING MURALS PARK

The park depicts 8 nos. Classical dance form and 4nos. Folk dance form from different parts of India.

ORNAMENTAL STRUCTURAL BRIDGE

It is constructed for the sole purpose of beautification of the Canal between 2 no. road and 3 no. road at Gangasagar.
