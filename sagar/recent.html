Special initiatives in2017-18

10 Mouzas of Sagar Development Block and 9 Mouzas of Namkhana Development Block are being added to this Dev. Authority. Proposal under consideration. Mackintosh Burn Ltd., a State PSU has been engaged for preparation of a Comprehensive Development Plan for Gangasagar and Bakkhali. Report to be submitted by 20th September, 2017. Scope of Work will be:

  • To Safeguard the Coastline.
  • Beachfront development and beautification.
  • Solid and Liquid Waste Management.
  • To Develop infrastructural facility for Tourism- Development of Eco-Tourism preserving the Bio-Diversity of the region.
  • To develop infrastructures for amenities for local people like drinking water, roads, etc.
  • To increase livelihood options of local people by involving them in different activities relating to Tourism.
  • To Bridge Critical Gaps in coordination with other departments.

Installation of Any Time Water (ATW) Booths in Tourist Spots, Market places and other places of Public gathering. To Cover all Schools and ICDS Centres with Running Water facilities.

To Complete Land Use & Development Control Plan (LUDCP) of 10 Mouzas by 2017-18 To construct additional Permanent Toilet Blocks Sewage Treatment Plant at different strategic points for Gangasagar Mela Makeover of Gangasagar Tourist Lodge will be done by Hooghly River Bridge Commissioners (HRBC) Gangasagar- Bakkhali- Jambudwip Tourism Circuit to be made Functional by December, 2017 through Jambudwip Nature Trail Trip.
