SEMINARS

  • National Seminar on Indic Perspective on International Relations was organized by the Department of Political Science and International Relations of Gautam Buddha University, Greater Noida with the collaboration of CSIR on 1st and 2nd February 2019. The Seminar was sponsored by the ICSSR, and Indian Oil, New Delhi.

    • The Seminar was attended by luminaries from the academic and policy world. The hosts comprising the Gautam Buddha University Vice-Chancellor, Prof. Bhagwati Prakash Sharma, and the Convener, Dr Vivek Kumar Mishra, welcomed the dignitaries. Shri Sunil Ji Ambekar, National Organising Secretary of Akhil Bhartiya Vidhyarthi Parishad (ABVP), delivered the inaugural speech on 01 February 2019.

  • CSIR American Studies Meet

After due deliberations and with the guidance and supervision of Prof. Ashwini Sir and Dr Rajeev Nayan, with unstinting efforts by Dr Abhishek, Dr Manan and Dr Vivek, the meet started its academic activities under the banner of CSIR. The American group has organized six webinars and physical conferences. The twin themes of the order of publishing an edited Book, titled "About America" by KW publishers, were discussed threadbare, along with any scholar volunteering to write a monograph on any topical and pertinent theme involving the United States of America.
